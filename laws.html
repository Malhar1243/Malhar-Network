Laws & Punishment

Follow the rules to keep Friends SMP fair, fun, and friendly.

Server Laws
1. No griefing, stealing, or destroying builds
2. No hacking, cheating, or unfair mods
3. No exploiting bugs or glitches
4. Follow staff instructions at all times
Punishments
Minor rule break Warning / Mute
Repeated minor offenses Temporary Mute / Jail
Griefing / Stealing Temporary Ban
Hacking / X-ray / Cheats Permanent Ban
Important Note
Punishments are based on severity and intent. Staff may change the punishment. Staff decisions are final.
